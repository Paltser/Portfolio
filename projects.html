Projects

Recycle!!!

An indie game that was made in Unity to spread environmental awareness. In that game, you play as a garbage collector and your job is to sort all the random trash spawning throughout the world, each time you sort correctly you get a point and wrongly sorting will deduct that point. The game offers parkour mechanics and also a few Easter eggs.

Aastategija 2024

In the Aastategija 2024 Programming competition, students work in teams to create a website that meets the specific requirements of a client. This competition provided students with the opportunity to showcase their skills in web development, teamwork, and problem solving.

Voco Liikumine

The goal of the project is to create an online application that would make it easier for teachers of the sports department in Tartu Vocational College to organize training sessions and for students to follow them. The project is still under development and is expected to launch in early April 2024
